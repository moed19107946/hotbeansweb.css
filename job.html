Job Description Information

Web Designer

Usng HTML, CSS, and other web design tools to create interesting and user-friendly websites. - Work together with senior developers and designers to produce web interfaces that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. - Help with the production of prototypes, mockups, and wireframes to showcase design concepts. Make sure webpages are optimised and responsive across a range of devices and browsers. - Remain current with web development best practices and design trends. Participate in the whole design process and offer original ideas to improve the user experience. - Capacity to efficiently fulfil deadlines while working on several tasks at once. - A keen eye for detail and a love of producing designs that are visually appealing..

Web Developer

Support the creation and upkeep of websites and web applications by utilising HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other programming languages. Work together with the development team to put the features and functionality of the website into action. Investigate and fix problems to make sure webpages function properly on various browsers and devices. - To increase the quality of your code, learn and use coding standards and best practices. - Take part in code reviews and support ongoing efforts to enhance web development procedures. - To improve abilities and knowledge, keep abreast with new developments in web development and technology. - Perform back-end and front-end development activities as required. - The capacity to adjust to shifting project requirements and work in a fast-paced environment...

Digital marketing

Support the creation and execution of digital marketing initiatives on many web platforms. - Work along with the marketing team to provide interesting content for email campaigns, websites, and social media. - Use tools like Google Analytics to track and evaluate the results of digital marketing campaigns. - Encourage the development of SEO tactics to raise organic traffic and website visibility. - Acquire and implement digital advertising best practices, such as social media and PPC campaigns. - Keep abreast of industry advancements and trends in digital marketing to bring new ideas to the table for the team. - Help with social media account management and online community interaction to increase brand recognition. - Capacity to efficiently fulfil deadlines while working on several tasks at once.
